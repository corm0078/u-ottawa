Win2020

Ecommerce

Yard Truck Specialist is a leading seller in Yard Truck Parts and Ottawa Kalmar Trucks. They were looking for an online part commerce for their business. Unlike a traditional e-commerce, they did not have product images for most of their parts. The most important part of this project was to establish an easy way for the customers to locate the parts required.

We came up with a design that looked more like a catalogue than a traditional e-commerce store. The search bar replaced the traditional slider on the homepage, allowing the user to find the parts they needed right away. We also had to create two different product pages, in order to showcase products with images and without.
