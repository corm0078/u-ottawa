Win2020

Ecommerce

The Win2020 is a non-profit fundraiser for 2 local hospitals. This fundraiser featured a 2020 Camaro as the grand prize as well as 2 early bird prices. We had to create a one-page Ecommerce site that would allow users to buy tickets.

We created a simple webpage that made it simple for users to purchase tickets in three steps - add to cart - cart and checkout. We made it simple for users to understand the prizes they could win and cause they would be supporting with their purchase. Overall we kept the site simple and direct and made a very successful fundraising campaign.
