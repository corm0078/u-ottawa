Tea Expert

App Design, UX

David's Tea is a Canadian loose leaf tea brand, with a large selection of tea types and flavours. As an avid tea drinker, I've always been a fan of their products. Making tea at home has always been difficult as it is hard to keep track of the different brewing times for all the various teas. Therefore, I created an app that would streamline the process.

The user interface makes it easy for the user to create the perfect tea. By selecting the amount, the type and the strength of the tea, the user gets an easy to follow guide. The app also makes it easy to brew your next batch by favouriting your teas on hand.
